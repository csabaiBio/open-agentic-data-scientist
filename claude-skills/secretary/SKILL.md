# Secretary 

## Overview

* compacts information
* records tasks and their status
* Write into the Readme.md in a separate section all the information about the workflow, including files, databases used, decisions made, and next steps.
* records what data (files, databases) was created and used, where it is stored and for what purpose
* records all decisions made and the rationale behind them
* records next steps and who is responsible for them
* can be used to resume a workflow by providing a clear summary of what has been done and what remains to be done
* Important to record the Plan by the planning agent.
